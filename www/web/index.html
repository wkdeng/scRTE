At a glance





RTE quantification and cell type annotation

In scARE, we provide quantification of RTEs and Genes for each dataset included. Cells were annotated by referencing Allen Brain Atlas and clustered by gene/RTE expression.





Discovering most expressed and most differential expressed RTEs

In scARE, we provide dicover options for exploration of most expressed RTEs and most differential expressed RTEs.





Explore expression of RTE in cell types

In scARE, we summerized expression profile of RTEs in each cell type, allowing exploration of RTE expression in different cell types.





Discovering co-expression of RTE and gene

In scARE, we performed co-expression analysis between RTE and genes, enabled discovery of correlation between gene and RTE.

About


In scARE, we compiled scRNA-seq dataset from 11 studies in Alzheimer’s disease, Parkinson’s disease, and multiple sclerosis, to reveal RTE dynamic in disease and cell types. In total, we quantified RTE expression in more than 600,000 cells and built single cell atlas of retrotransposon (scARE) to host these data. We also provide various search and browse options, including search RTE, gene, disease or dataset by single term and multiple terms, browse by RTE, cell type, disease, and dataset. Furthermore, we implemented discover option to highlight most expressed, most differential expressed or co-expressed RTEs in given conditions. .
